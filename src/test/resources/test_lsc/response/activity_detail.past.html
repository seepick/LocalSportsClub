Details:

Multi-instrumental Sound Bath with Katty Heath Sound Healing soothes and calms the nervous system. It helps decrease stress, anxiety, insomnia and physical pain by reconnecting us with the sounds of nature and pure acoustic vibrations. During a session the listener lies down, eyes closed, to be washed in a sea of different sounds from various beautiful instruments, such as singing bowls, flute, rain stick, kalimba, shamanic drum, bells, chimes and the human voice. The recipient can enter a deep meditative state, where they may experience clarity, release, balancing of chakra centres, and much more. Katty Heath - BioKatty is a singer and sound therapist from the UK.She studied contemporary music at university and has been a professional vocalist, voice coach and piano teacher for over 20 years. In 2019 she completed her training in Sound Therapy at Sound Healing Bali. Since then she has been facilitating sound baths to groups or private sessions at studios, festivals and retreats worldwide, including Ibiza, India, Slovakia, France and Germany.
